Why Unified Liquidity Matters

Fragmented markets mean fragmented pricing. When liquidity is scattered across dozens of venues, traders pay wider spreads, suffer more slippage, and miss opportunities.

T1 Broker's Smart Order Router continuously scans 30+ exchanges, decentralized protocols, and dark pools to find the best price at any given moment. Your order is split and routed optimally across venues in under a millisecond.

The result: tighter spreads, deeper fills, and zero fragmentation tax.

For crypto assets, T1 sources liquidity directly from on-chain AMMs and aggregators across 13 blockchains, combining CEX depth with DeFi composability — all settled through our institutional-grade clearing engine.
