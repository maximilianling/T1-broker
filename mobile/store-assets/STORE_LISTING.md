Stream live prices for thousands of instruments. View interactive candlestick charts, order books, and market depth all in real time.

Place market, limit, and stop orders with a streamlined two-step flow. Quick-fill buttons and haptic feedback make trading fast and intuitive.

Portfolio Analytics
Track your portfolio performance with beautiful interactive charts. View P&L, allocation breakdowns, and historical performance across multiple time periods.

Bank-Grade Security
Protect your account with biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID), two-factor authentication (TOTP, email codes), trusted device management, and login risk scoring.

Deposit and withdraw funds seamlessly. Track all transfers with a detailed transaction history.

Set custom price alerts that trigger push notifications when your conditions are met — above, below, or when a price crosses your target.

Watchlists
Build personalized watchlists to monitor the instruments you care about most.
